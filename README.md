# Amazon-E-commerce-Sales-Analytics-Dashboard(Power BI)

📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive E-commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI. The dashboard analyzes Amazon product data to uncover insights about sales performance, product categories, pricing strategies, and customer ratings.

The objective of this project is to demonstrate data cleaning, data modeling, and business intelligence skills by transforming raw product data into meaningful insights through interactive visualizations.

🎯 Business Problem

E-commerce platforms manage thousands of products across different categories. Understanding which products perform well, how pricing strategies affect customer engagement, and how customer ratings influence demand is essential for business decision-making.

This dashboard helps answer key business questions such as:

Which product categories generate the highest sales?

How do product discounts affect customer engagement?

Which products receive the most customer reviews?

What is the overall customer satisfaction level?

🔍 Key Insights

Some insights derived from the dashboard include:

Electronics and home appliance categories dominate the product listings.

Products with higher discounts tend to attract more customer engagement.

Most products have ratings above 4.0, indicating high customer satisfaction.

A small number of products generate the majority of customer reviews.
